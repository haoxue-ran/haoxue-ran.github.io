## Biography

I am Ran Haoxue (冉浩学), a Ph.D. candidate majoring in Environmental Science and Engineering at the School of Environmental and Municipal Engineering, **Xi'an University of Architecture and Technology (XAUAT)**. I conduct my research within the academic team of **Prof. Rui Miao (苗瑞)** and collaborate closely with **Associate Researcher Jin Zhao (赵进)** from Xi'an Jiaotong University. 

My expected graduation date is **June 2027**. Currently, I am actively seeking international **Postdoctoral positions** starting in 2027 to further pursue my research passions in advanced water treatment and environmental data science.

## Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of **Membrane-based Water Treatment Technology** and **Data-Driven Frameworks (Artificial Intelligence/Machine Learning)**. I focus on utilizing interpretable machine learning, causal inference, and molecular dynamics simulations to analyze organic membrane fouling mechanisms. My core technical expertise and research areas include:

*   **Membrane Fouling Mechanisms**: Quantifying interfacial interactions and developing prevention strategies.
*   **Machine Learning & Causal Inference**: Utilizing interpretable machine learning models (e.g., CatBoost, XGBoost) to decouple complex foulant-membrane interactions.
*   **Computational Simulations**: Employing Multi-scale Molecular Dynamics (MD) Simulations and Density Functional Theory (DFT) to gain microscopic molecular insights.
*   **Resource Recovery**: Developing novel strategies and studying interfacial crystallization mechanisms for lithium extraction adsorbents in salt lakes.
